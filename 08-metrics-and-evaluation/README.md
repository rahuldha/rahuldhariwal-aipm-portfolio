## The Core Principle

Every AI product needs three layers of measurement:
1. **Model metrics** — is the AI doing the cognitive task correctly?
2. **Product metrics** — is the AI driving the intended user behavior?
3. **Business metrics** — is the AI moving the outcome that justified the investment?

Measuring only one layer misleads. Containment rate without resolution rate produces a system that "handles" interactions without solving them. Model accuracy without CSAT delta produces a system that's technically correct but user-hostile.
