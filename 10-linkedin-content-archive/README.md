## Content Strategy

**Target audience**: AI PMs, technical PMs transitioning to AI, engineering managers evaluating AI products, recruiters at AI-first companies.

**Positioning**: Production AI practitioner with fintech depth — not an AI theorist or a demo builder.

**Content pillars**:
1. Lessons from production AI (what worked, what didn't, why)
2. AI PM frameworks (how to think about AI product decisions)
3. AI product teardowns (public analysis of successful AI products)
4. Fintech + AI intersection (regulatory, compliance, underserved users)
